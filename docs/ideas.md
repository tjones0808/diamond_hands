# Career Roguelite Trader Ideas

## Current Sprint

- Stock Intel: make each ticker feel like a tradable character with signal quality, volatility, price move, and a tiny tape read.
- Trade Ticket: replace one-click buttons with size controls, cost/risk estimates, and clearer buy/sell actions.
- Weekly Goals: show the promotion gate as concrete week objectives so every run has a near-term target.

## Backlog

- Options Chaos Upgrade: add contracts with DTE, delta-like risk, premium decay, and bigger Friday swing stories.
- Win/Loss Feedback: show payoff bursts, bad-beat notes, and lessons after major trades or expiry.
- Career Progression Rewards: unlock new venues, instruments, clients, leverage, and pressure as tiers change.
- Room Upgrade Effects: make monitors, news feeds, terminals, and offices change both visuals and mechanics.
- Risk/Mood System: add stress, confidence, and reputation pressure that changes trader decisions.
- Roguelite Meta: keep best run, career badges, desk trophies, and permanent knowledge unlocks.
- Onboarding First Week: tutorialize read, build, shock, risk, expiry without slowing down repeat runs.
- Market Personality: give tickers recurring identities so players build favorites and grudges.
- Rival Traders: add named rivals on the leaderboard who react to your biggest wins and blowups.
- Endgame Wall Street Floor: turn the final high-rise into a dense command center with institutional-scale trades.
